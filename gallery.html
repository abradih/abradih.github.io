The process

Punky

Shortly before Thanksgiving 2016 my Dad's dog Punky (my childhood dog) came to the end of his life. Punky had been with me for some of the hardest times of my life and was my Dad's little buddy. With that in mind I knew my Dad would appreciate a painting of Punky and I tried my hand at oil paints. I gave the resulting painting to my Dad for Christmas.
