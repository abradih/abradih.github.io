Prices are the base price to give you an idea of how much a commission at that size, detail and medium will cost. Shipping is NOT included in the price.

There are two options for background, either vague details from the original photo (good if dog is positioned on an object) or a white or black background. I can do a second pet in the same portrait for an addition of half the original cost, however; they must be in the same photo together. I can't merge two photos together.

Oil Painting

Oil paintings are mounted in a wood frame and can be hung on frame. Oil paintings require more time for the paint to set, which can take up to a week.

Detailed Portait

I catch all the fine details of your pet, down to the texture of their fur, feathers or scales.

Basic Sketch
