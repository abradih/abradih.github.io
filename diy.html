Starting mid-February Ben and I have started a metal casting class at the Workshop a workspace located at Iowa State University. The first week we were to find an object to cast the only requirement was that it wasn’t to be made of metal. I took little laser cut wood gears from the hobby store and glued them together to make a pattern that I liked. I had planned on using it as a decorative piece or I will solder it to a hair clip to use in my hair. Ben decided to use one of his rubber ducky’s that sits on top of his computer monitor. If your curious why he has a rubber ducky on his monitor go to rubber duck debugging to find out.

After picking our objects we began the process to turn our objects into metal.
